Senior Product Designer, Enterprise XD

Location: USA Remote (Quarterly travel required)

Salary: $120,000 - $515,000 (Based on experience and location)

The Role

Netflix is one of the world’s leading entertainment services with 283 million paid memberships in over 190 countries enjoying TV series, films, and games across a wide variety of genres and languages. Members can play, pause and resume watching as much as they want, anytime, anywhere, and can change their plans at any time.

As a Senior Designer, you will work at multiple levels—from strategic to tactical—helping to define the team’s strategy and vision, to rapidly sketching and testing prototypes, to working with engineering to refine features. You must have empathy and deeply understand our global users, in order to create intuitive experiences that make it easy to get the job done. Netflix's Enterprise Experience Design team focuses on developing products to help plan, produce, launch, and promote the best content for global members.

What You’ll Do

  • Partner closely with multi-disciplinary teams (Product Design, Content Design, Engineering, Research, etc.) to take the business to the next level.
  • Work across all stages of the design process (user research, ideation, concept development, validation, wireframing, prototyping, final visual design, and implementation support).
  • Create beautiful, emotionally compelling, pixel-perfect mockups and prototypes.
  • Conduct usability testing and iterate on designs with users.
  • Partner with PMs to craft systematic solutions that connect user needs, data, and business goals.
  • Partner with engineers to build impactful products and features within technological and timeline constraints.
  • Facilitate collaborative work sessions with all levels of company stakeholders.

The Must-Haves

  • 5+ years of experience in enterprise software product design.
  • Track record of designing and shipping enterprise or B2B experiences.
  • Experience navigating ambiguity and driving clarity into actionable roadmaps.
  • Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Mastery of Figma’s advanced features (Autolayout, Variables, DevMode, etc.).
  • Experience collaborating with machine learning and data science teams.

When Evaluating Your Experience and Skills, We Will Focus On

  • An outstanding portfolio showcasing your strengths in visual design craft, interaction design, and rationale behind your design decisions.
  • Effective communication and presentation skills.
  • Strong conceptual thinking balancing user needs, business goals, and feasibility.
  • Effective time management skills to balance project schedule with fast-paced deadlines.
